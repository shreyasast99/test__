Climate Change

Global warming is an extremely important problem that needs to be addressed immediately and in a drastic way. It can cause ice caps to melt resulting in smaller shorelines and severe flooding. This sharp increase in temperature can be the cause of harsh droughts that persist for long periods of time. For example, California is going through the worst drought in 1200 years because of global warming. Acute warming disrupts coral ecosystem and precipitates animal extinction and can contribute to the extinction of well known species such as polar bears and seals who depend on the ice caps to survive and catch prey. In addition to these terrible consequences, increases in temperature can precipitate a breeding ground for pathogens and vectors that carry them. Global warming is a rampant problem in our world today, and it is our duty to push awareness for it.
